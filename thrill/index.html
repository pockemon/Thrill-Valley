About

Thrill Valley

Thrill Valley, located in a picturesque location in Yelagiri hill is your go-to destination for adventure in south India. Ideally placed between Bangalore and Chennai, Thrill Valley makes an ideal weekend getaway destination.
Thrill Valley offer over 40 plus adventure sports. Many are unique and for the first time in India. From pony rides to adrenaline pumping kart races. We have a wide range of activities for every member of your family.
We also provide home stay and camping facilities in our premise. Check out Aura Valley for more details.
